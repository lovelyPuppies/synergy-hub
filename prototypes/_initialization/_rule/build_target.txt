  Target Triple naming (target_triple)
    üìÖ Written at 2025-02-04 09:37:52
    ‚û°Ô∏è Format: <arch><sub>-<vendor>-<sys>-<env>
      - "<sub>" and "<vendor>" can be omitted üîò unless: (OR condition)
        - they affect ABI
